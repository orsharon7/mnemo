Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

We collect nothing.

Mnemo does not collect, transmit, or share any data about you or your clipboard contents. Everything stays on your Mac.

What stays on your device

  • Your clipboard history is stored locally in ~/Library/Application Support/Mnemo.
  • No account is required. No sign-in screen. No cloud sync.
  • Mnemo makes zero network requests.

Permissions

Mnemo runs with zero permissions by default. The only optional permission is Accessibility, required only if you enable auto-paste. No other permission is requested or used.

Open source

Mnemo is fully open source. You can audit the code at github.com/orsharon7/mnemo.
